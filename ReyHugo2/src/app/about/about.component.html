ABOUT

Rey Hugo

Rahil Huguley was born in Canton, Ohio in 1980, and raised in Columbus, Ohio. He is married with 3 children. A graduate of Ohio Christian University, Rahil has self-published his first book "The 5 Secrets To Living Life" and released over 25 inspirational albums independently since 2013. Rahil has been involved in ministry since the age of 13. Rahil Huguley desires to inspire others to love themselves, use their gifts, and follow their dreams.
